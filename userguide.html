Model: OnePlus 8 5G "Hardened Edition" | Storage: All-Weather Vault

1. Initial Setup (Do This Now)

  • Full Charge: Connect your Solar Power Bank to a wall outlet using a high-quality brick. Charge until all 4 LEDs are solid (100%).
  • Phone Health: Power on the OnePlus 8 5G. Ensure it is at ~50% charge (ideal for long-term storage).
  • Seal the Vault: Place the phone and battery into their respective Mission Darkness Faraday Bags. Fold the top twice and seal the Velcro tightly. Place both inside the MEIJIA Case with the included Silica Gel packets.

2. Long-Term Maintenance (The 6-Month Rule)

To ensure this kit works years from now, you must perform a maintenance check twice a year:

  • Open the case and remove devices from the Faraday bags.
  • Top off the battery: Plug the power bank into a wall outlet until full.
  • Check the phone: Power it on, let it run for 10 minutes, then power it completely off at 50% charge.
  • Reseal everything.
  • Mark your calendar.

3. Emergency Deployment (Grid-Down Scenario)

  • Signal Silence: Once removed from the Faraday bag, the phone will attempt to find a signal. Keep Airplane Mode ON at all times to prevent the battery from draining while searching for dead towers.

Accessing Knowledge:

  • Maps: Open the "Organic Maps" or "OsmAnd" app for 100% offline GPS navigation.
  • AI: Open the "Local LLM" app to chat with the AI for medical, mechanical, or tactical advice.
  • Wiki: Open "Kiwix" to access the full offline Wikipedia and Medical database.

4. Solar Charging Logic

Warning: The solar panel is for emergency trickle-charging only.

  • Place the Solar Power Bank in direct, overhead sunlight.
  • Angle the panel toward the sun.
  • Do not leave the phone connected while solar charging in high heat; the heat can damage the phone's battery.

Expectations: 1 hour of direct sun may provide enough power for a short emergency call or 15 minutes of AI use.

5. EMP Protocol (Pro Kit Only)

If an EMP or Solar Flare is suspected:

  • Immediate Action: Place all electronics in the Mission Darkness Faraday Bags immediately.
  • The Double Fold: The seal is only effective if the top is folded over itself at least twice before the Velcro is engaged.
  • Isolation: Do not let the metal parts of the devices touch the inner lining of the bag if possible; use the included protective sleeves.
